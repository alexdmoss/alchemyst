- 1 - REACTIVE INTERMEDIATES NOTES Carbenes These are neutral, divalent, highly reactive carbon intermediates. The two non- bonding electrons can be arranged parallel or spin-paired. Spin-paired show both electrons an sp2 orbital, leaving the p orbital vacant. This is the singlet state. The triplet state shows both electrons with parallel spins and leaves one electron in an sp2 orbital and the other in a p orbital, and this is the triplet state.
